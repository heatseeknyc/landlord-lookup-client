About the NYC Landlord Lookup tool

The NYC Landlord Lookup tool was created to provide easy access to 3 major open data sets regarding property ownership which are of general interest to NYC residents, but which (as of early 2016) are not easily accessible via the City's websites. It is an open source project sponsored by Heat Seek NYC and maintained by volunteers.

Correctness and Completeness

Best efforts have been made to present the city's datasets as-is, and importantly, to avoid innacurate record matches. That said, the city's datasets themselves are, we have found, simply broken in various ways for a small but significant portion of properties (around 1-2% for each source, compounding to about 5% across all sources combined). To the extent that we've been able to identify this "brokenness", we've chosen to omit certain properties and buildings from our searches (rather than return false or ambiguous matches).

So if you've typed an address that you're pretty sure corresponds to a property you know about directly, but aren't gettting at least lat/lon hit and basic ownership status, that's the most likely reason. Specific datasets also have gaps in coverage, as detailed in the section on Data Sources above.
